DTES - Ad Hoc Email is a temporary disposable email service. Its public website provides a disposable email service for the domain @temp.ootech.vn.
You can send an email to any @temp.ootech.vn address and check the DTES mailbox to retrieve and read the email.

But DTES is also installable as an on premise server, serving your own domains.
Setting up DTES as your own temporary email service is now ever easier with Docker.
